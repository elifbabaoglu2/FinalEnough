Purpose of this portfolio
I believe that a meaningful user experience is conducive to business success. Therefore, I make the user’s needs my priority. I create one-page applications that are easy to use and eliminate navigation issues tied to multiple-page applications. I use colors and textures to keep the application exciting and minimize routing to keep the application simplistic.
Conclusion
I realized the importance of usability principles with this course. There is always a tradeoff between different design choices. My one page applications are easy to use. However, visual simplicity is being traded to minimize routing. As a designer, I believe that usage simplicity is more important than visual simplicity, and I make my design decisions based on this view.

A/B Testing

Purpose

The goal of this assignment is to perform an A/B test and statistical analysis to gather insight into two different UI designs.

Take Aways

I concluded that having a variety of colors yields a longer attention span and makes the application more successful. My conclusion was based on our null hypothesis. And the aggregated data was collected from 15 people, which was more than the minimum needed sample size.
